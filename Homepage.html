Bilkent CS491/2 Project
Stock Vision

Stock Vision is a mobile application which analyzes past stock/etf values, and creates a real time simulation for users. The goal of our project is to provide a realistic simulation for our users to practice their investment skills with fake currency in our application, and recieve real life solutions and tips from our AI.
